# __1\. Introduction__

1\.1 This Return and Refund Policy \(the “Policy”\) explains how returns, refunds, and order cancellations work on Hive, available at https://hivenow\.in \(the “Platform”\), operated by BEELYN LLP, a limited liability partnership having LLPIN ACS\-4901 and its registered office at 55/4379, Door No\. 3623, Valanjambalam Junction, Kochi M\.G\. Road, Ernakulam, Kerala – 682016, India \(“BEELYN LLP,” “Hive,” “we,” “us,” or “our”\)\.

1\.2 This Policy forms an integral part of, and should be read together with, the Hive Customer Terms and Conditions, Version 2\.0 \(“Customer Terms”\) and the Privacy Policy\. In the event of any inconsistency between this Policy and the Customer Terms, the Customer Terms shall prevail\.

1\.3 This Policy applies to purchases made on the Platform from independent boutiques, designer studios, and fashion sellers \(“Sellers”\)\. Hive operates as a marketplace facilitator and coordinates the return and refund process on behalf of Customers and Sellers, as described in this Policy\.

# __2\. Return Window__

2\.1 Customers wishing to initiate a return must generally do so within __two \(2\) days from the date of delivery__ of the Product\.

2\.2 Return requests raised after expiry of this window may not be accepted, except where required under applicable consumer protection law\.

2\.3 To initiate a return, Customers should raise a request through the Platform's “My Orders” section or contact support@hivenow\.in within the return window stated above\.

# __3\. No Change\-of\-Mind Returns__

3\.1 Hive is committed to keeping returns fair for both Customers and the independent boutiques selling on the Platform\. Returns are therefore __not accepted__ for:

\(a\) change of mind after purchase;

\(b\) change in personal preference;

\(c\) no longer wanting or needing the Product\.

# __4\. Eligible Returns__

4\.1 Subject to this Policy, return requests may be accepted only where:

\(a\) the Product delivered is __physically damaged__;

\(b\) a __different Product__ from the one ordered has been delivered;

\(c\) the Product is __materially defective__, and the defect is not attributable to Customer use or mishandling;

\(d\) a __size discrepancy__ exists, as described in Clause 5 below\.

# __5\. Size\-Related Returns__

5\.1 A return on the basis of size may be accepted where:

\(a\) the Seller delivers a size that is different from the size expressly ordered by the Customer; or

\(b\) the actual garment measurements of the delivered Product substantially differ from the measurements represented in the product description or size chart\.

5\.2 In assessing size\-related return claims, Hive will act reasonably and in good faith, taking into account the nature and category of the Product, any manufacturing tolerances disclosed or customary for that product category, and the accuracy of the information presented in the listing at the time of purchase\.

5\.3 Where a Customer has ordered an incorrect size despite the Seller having provided accurate size information in the listing, Hive may decline the return request\.

# __6\. Unboxing Video Requirement__

6\.1 For claims involving damaged products, defective products, missing items, or wrong products delivered, Hive may request supporting evidence, including a __continuous, uninterrupted unboxing video__ recorded from the moment the outer packaging is opened\.

6\.2 This requirement helps prevent fraudulent claims, protects genuine Customers, and enables faster resolution of legitimate issues\. Failure to provide reasonable supporting evidence when requested may result in the return claim being declined\.

6\.3 This requirement does not exclude or override any statutory rights available to Customers under applicable consumer protection law\.

# __6\. How to Raise a Return__

6\.1 To raise a return, Customers should:

\(a\) go to “My Orders” on the Platform and select the relevant Order within the two \(2\) day return window;

\(b\) choose the applicable reason for return from the options provided;

\(c\) upload supporting evidence where requested, including photographs;

\(d\) submit the request for review\.

6\.2 Hive will review the request and notify the Customer of the outcome, including next steps for return pickup where the claim is approved\.

# __7\. Return Shipping and Pickup__

7\.1 Where a return claim is approved, Hive will arrange for pickup of the Product and will generally bear the cost of return logistics\.

7\.2 Customers are requested to keep the Product in its original condition, with original tags and packaging intact, until pickup is completed\.

# __8\. Refund Timeline__

8\.1 The return window for raising a claim is __two \(2\) days from the date of delivery__, as set out in Clause 2\.

8\.2 Once a return is approved and the returned Product has been picked up and, where applicable, verified, Hive will process the refund to the Customer’s original payment source\. Refunds are generally credited within __five \(5\) to seven \(7\) business days__ of approval\.

8\.3 The actual time for the refunded amount to reflect in the Customer’s account or card statement may vary depending on the Customer’s bank or payment provider and is not within Hive’s control\.

8\.4 Where an Order is cancelled prior to dispatch — for example, where a Seller does not accept the Order within the applicable acceptance window, or a Customer cancels prior to Seller acceptance — any amount paid will generally be refunded within __five \(5\) to seven \(7\) business days__\.

# __9\. Order Cancellations__

9\.1 Customers may request cancellation of an Order prior to Seller acceptance\. Once an Order has been accepted by the Seller, cancellation may not be possible except as set out in the Customer Terms or as required by applicable law\.

9\.2 If a Seller does not accept or reject an Order within the applicable acceptance window, Hive may automatically cancel the Order and will notify the Customer of the cancellation and its reason\. Any amount paid will be refunded in accordance with Clause 9\.

# __10\. Refunds for Payment Failures__

10\.1 Where a payment is debited but an Order is not successfully confirmed due to a payment failure, reversal, or suspected fraud, Hive will endeavour to ensure the amount is returned to the Customer’s original payment source within __five \(5\) to seven \(7\) business days__\.

# __11\. Non\-Returnable Situations__

11\.1 In addition to change\-of\-mind returns described in Clause 3, Hive may decline a return where:

\(a\) the return request is raised after the two \(2\) day return window;

\(b\) the Product shows signs of use, alteration, or damage not present at the time of delivery;

\(c\) original tags, packaging, or accessories are missing, where applicable to the claim;

\(d\) requested supporting evidence, including photographs, is not provided;

\(e\) the claimed size discrepancy falls within reasonable manufacturing tolerances and the listing information was accurate, as described in Clause 5\.

# __12\. Statutory Rights Unaffected__

12\.1 Nothing in this Policy excludes, restricts, or limits any rights available to Customers under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, the Consumer Protection \(E\-Commerce\) Rules, 2020, or any other applicable consumer protection law in India\.

12\.2 Where any provision of this Policy is inconsistent with a Customer’s statutory rights, the statutory right shall prevail\.
